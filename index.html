Our Story

Two friends.
One dream.

C&C was born from a friendship, a shared passion, and way too many hours spent talking about hair over cold coffee. Jordan and Aurora met in cosmetology school, bonded over their obsession with color theory and texture work, and never stopped dreaming about having a space of their own.

Today that dream is a studio where you can come exactly as you are, leave feeling like yourself again, and never once sit under fluorescent lights while a stranger asks if you want "just a trim."
